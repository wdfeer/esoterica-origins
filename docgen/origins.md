## Child of Miare
Preserver of knowledge, fragile in body yet blessed with memory and utility.

- Transmigration of Hieda: You keep items and experience on death.
- Perfect Memory: You gain 50% more experience.
- Scroll Crafting: You can craft books without using leather.
- Diminishing Lifespan: Lose 1 heart permanently and prevent death every 60 minutes, up to 5 hearts.
